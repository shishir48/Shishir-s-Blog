Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 12, 2026. This policy explains what data Local Legend collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

What is Local Legend

Local Legend is a mobile app that helps you discover high-quality local businesses in cities you select. You can browse curated listings, read business details, and find hidden gems in your area.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — name and email address when you register.
  • City selection — the city you choose to browse businesses in. We do not track your precise GPS location.
  • Usage data — which screens you visit and features you use, to help us improve the app.
  • Device information — device type and operating system version for troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

  • Show you business listings relevant to your selected city.
  • Maintain and improve app performance and reliability.
  • Send optional account-related notifications (you can opt out at any time).
  • Detect and prevent abuse or fraudulent activity.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access is restricted to authorised personnel only. No system is completely immune to risk; we will notify you promptly if a breach affects your data.

Third-Party Services

Local Legend may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services — for app distribution and updates.
  • Analytics tools — to understand aggregate usage patterns. No personally identifiable data is shared.

Children's Privacy

Local Legend is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.
  • Export your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notice or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
